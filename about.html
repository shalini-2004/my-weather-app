Key Features

Current Weather & Location Detection:

Our app automatically detects your location to provide real-time weather updates. If you'd prefer, you can manually search for weather information in any city worldwide.

Global Time Zones:

Stay connected with global weather patterns by viewing weather information adjusted to different time zones. This feature is especially useful for frequent travelers or those coordinating with others across the globe.

5-Day Forecast:

Plan your week with confidence by accessing our 5-day weather forecast, which gives you a clear overview of upcoming weather conditions.

Dynamic Backgrounds:

Experience an immersive user interface with backgrounds that change based on the time of day and the weather in the selected location, enhancing your interaction with the app.

User-Friendly Design:

Our app is designed for simplicity and ease of use. The intuitive interface ensures that you can quickly access the information you need without any hassle.

Detailed Weather Insights:

Beyond the basics, our app provides in-depth weather details such as humidity levels, wind speeds, visibility, and sunrise/sunset times. We also include information about the UV index, helping you stay aware of potential sun exposure risks.
